About Us

Bringing Nature into Your Home

We design and craft custom wooden interiors to elevate your space.

Our Story

Backwood Interiors is a unique endeavor, owned and operated by a team of talented and dedicated women who share a deep connection with both craftsmanship and nature. In addition to their passion for creating stunning spaces, they also manage a thriving farm, further embodying their commitment to sustainable living and holistic harmony with the environment. This distinctive blend of skill, creativity, and a close connection to the land sets Backwood Interiors apart, making each piece of furniture a testament to their ingenuity and respect for nature.

Our Philosophy

Our furniture company takes pride in crafting exquisite pieces from ethically sourced wood. We blend the timeless beauty of wood with eco-conscious practices, bringing you furniture that not only enhances your living spaces but also contributes to the preservation of our natural surroundings.
